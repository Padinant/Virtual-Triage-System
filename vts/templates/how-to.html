How to Use This Tool

This guide will help you make use of the various features available on the website to get answers to your CSEE related questions.

How To Use The Homepage

On the homepage, you can use the search bar to search for frequently asked questions (FAQs). Click on the search bar, type in your question or keywords related to your question, and either click the "Search" button or press enter to see your relevant search results.

You can also view a list of FAQs directly on the homepage. Click on any questions in the grey box to popup the FAQ and see the answers. If you want to see the full FAQ page, click on the "Go to FAQ" button in the popup on the top left corner.

If you need further assistance, you can use the chatbot feature by clicking on the chat icon located at the bottom right corner of the page.

How To Browse FAQs

On the browse FAQ page, you can use the search bar to search for frequently asked questions (FAQs). Click on the search bar, type in your question or keywords related to your question, and either click the "Search" button or press enter to see your relevant search results.

You can also categorize the FAQs by selecting a category from the dropdown menu next to the search bar. This will filter the FAQs to show only those that belong to the selected category. You can also click on the "View All" button to see all FAQs without any filters.

If you need further assistance, you can use the chatbot feature by clicking on the chat icon located at the bottom right corner of the page.

How To Use The Chatbot

The chatbot feature is available on most pages of the website. There are two ways to access the chatbot: either by clicking on the "Ask Chatbot" link in the top menu or by clicking on the chat icon located at the bottom right corner of the page.

If you click on the "Ask Chatbot" link, you will be taken to a dedicated chatbot page where you can interact with the chatbot. Type your question into the prompt bar and either click the "Send" button or press enter to submit your question. The chatbot will respond with an appropriate answer. You can enable fullscreen mode by clicking the expand icon in the top right corner of the chat window for a larger view. Click the expand icon again to exit fullscreen mode.

If you click on the chat icon on the bottom right corner of the page, a chat widget will appear. Type your question into the prompt bar at the bottom of the widget and either click the "Send" button or press enter to submit your question. The chatbot will respond with an appropriate answer. You can also cycle through three different sizes of the widget by clicking the expand icon in the top right corner of the widget. You can close the widget by clicking the "X" icon next to the expand icon.

Other Options

The "Contact Us" link in the top menu will take you to the CSEE department's contact page, where you can find ways to get in touch with the department for further assistance.

The "Department Website" link in the top menu will take you to the official CSEE department website, where you can find more information about the department, its programs, and resources.
