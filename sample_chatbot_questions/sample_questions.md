This page includes a small list of plausible sample questions which you can ask the chatbot assistant. Most of these questions were also included in our video demo of the project.

Note that the chatbot has conversation history, meaning it remembers your previous chats.


# QUESTIONS TO ASK:

## Simple General Questions
Who are you? _should introduce itself_

What is UMBC? _should mention basic info about UMBC_


## Examples of Slightly Tricky Questions

Can you write me a poem about the CSEE department? _Should refuse. (an example of keeping to professional tone and writing style)_

Who is Amelia Earhart? _Should refuse. (an example of only answering relevant questions about the department)_

## Research Related Questions
I’m interested in Computer Graphics. Are there any research opportunities at UMBC? _(Should mention related professors to contact and likely the VANGOGH lab.)_

I want to get into Photonics Research. Where should I start? _Will likely mention related professors and labs (CASPR and Computational Photonics Laboratory) and possibly even related clubs (Photonics and Optoelectronics Club and the UMBC Quantum & Photonics Society)_

_You can ask similar questions about any of the research topics or areas listed on the CSEE department website_

Where is the CORAL lab located? _(Tells you where the CORAL lab is located, and give you some info about it)_

_You can ask similar questions about any of the research labs listed on the CSEE department website_

## Programs and Degree Questions
Can I do my undergrad and grad degree at once? _(Should talk about CSEE combined B.S./ M.S. programs)_

Does UMBC have any Cybersecurity certifications? _(Should go over the two cybersecurity certificates)_

What are my other options for studying in this field? _(This should demonstrate that the chatbot recalls history. It will likely talk about the different Cybersecurity tracks at UMBC for graduate and undergraduate study)_

I have some detailed questions about one of the undergraduate Computer Engineering programs at UMBC. Who can I contact? _(Should give you some helpful contact info)_

## Commonly Made Requests
How can I get card swipe access to a room? _(Should lay out the appropriate steps for doing this for both faculty and students. It should also provide the link to the faculty form which is called 'CSEE Room Access Request')_

How can I put a reservation request for a room, for my conference panel event? _(Should lay out steps for CSEE reservation requests and provide the link to the appropriate form which is called 'Room Reservation Request')_
